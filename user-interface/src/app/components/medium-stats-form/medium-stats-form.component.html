Medium post statistics Starts a background job that signs in to Medium (via saved session or credentials) and scrapes medium.com/me/stats. Results are stored as medium_stats_report.json on the job. For production, prefer server environment variables (MEDIUM_STORAGE_STATE_PATH or MEDIUM_EMAIL / MEDIUM_PASSWORD).
